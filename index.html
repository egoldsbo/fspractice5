Important notes:

there are over 3000 words in wordset named "inthewild". they were compiled from the dataset "Chicago Fingerspelling in the Wild Data Sets" more info about the sampleset is available here: https://home.ttic.edu/~klivescu/ChicagoFSWild.htm

This website provides buttons to look-up the fingerspelled word. keep in mind that ASL signs do not (usually) represent english words.

American Sign Language is its own natural language with its own words, grammar, culture etc....

for more information about ASL including free lessons I highly reccomend the lessons available for free at

https://www.lifeprint.com/asl101/lessons/lessons.htm

the list of words for the "most common signs" wordsets were taken from

https://www.handspeak.com/word/most-used/
